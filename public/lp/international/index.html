Frequently asked

FAQ

How do I order from overseas?

Visit our online store, pick your color and size, and check out securely. We ship worldwide — the entire process is in English.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes! Shipping starts at ~$5 USD for Southeast Asia and ~$8–12 USD for Japan, Korea, and Taiwan. Delivery takes 3–14 business days depending on your location. See our shipping table above for estimates.

What sizes do you offer?

Sizes XS–XL in both women's and men's fits, with a size guide provided. Not sure? Send us your measurements and we'll recommend a size.

How many colors are available?

18 shades — from SpearMint, Black Soul and Dark Sea to Wild Rose and more.

Can you embroider a logo or name?

Yes — perfect for individuals and teams. Send your logo or name when you chat and we'll arrange it.

How do I pay?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) and bank transfer through our online store at checkout.
